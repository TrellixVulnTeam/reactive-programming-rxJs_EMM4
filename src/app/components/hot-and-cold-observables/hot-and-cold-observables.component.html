Hot And Cold Observables

+ visual example

There are two types of observable, hot and cold.
Cold observables start to emit values only when we subscribe to them, when hot observables emit values always, dispite we have subscriber or not. Cold observables ar unicast, when hot observables are multicast (it means that observable share value between multiple subscribers).

In this case we emit random number from the observable.
